THANKS TO OUR SPONSORS

Mindcraft greatly appreciates all of our wonderful sponsors. Without their contribution, our team would not be able to grow as much as we did. They have allowed us to build and complete, as well as outreach to the community to promote stem education. Providing us with more materials, knowledge from mentors, and a higher budget, we have been able to continue with our program. Our sponsor levels are differentiated by how much is donated. Each level comes with different benefits to show our gratitude along with a tax write-off. The four sponsor levels include Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum.

Bronze

The Bronze Level is the fourth sponsor level. To receive this level, a minimum of $1,000 must be donated. The benefits include : a small sponsor logo on the Banner/Trailer/T-shirt, Team 3495 buttons, and a chance to request the robot’s appearance at 1 event.

Silver

The Silver Level is the third sponsor level. To receive this level, a minimum of $2,000 must be donated. The benefits include : a medium logo on the Banner/Trailer/T-shirt, Team 3495 buttons, and a chance to request the robot’s appearance at a few events.

Gold

The Gold Level is the second sponsor level. To receive this level, a minimum of $3,000 must be donated. The benefits include : a chance to name the robot for a year, a large logo on the Banner/Trailer/T-shirt, a chance to request the robot’s appearance any event, a miniature 3D printed robot, and Team 3495 buttons.

Platinum

The Platinum Level is the first sponsor level. To receive this level, a minimum of $4,000 must be donated. The benefits include : a chance to name the robot for a year, a large logo on the Banner/Trailer/T-shirt, a chance to request the robot’s appearance at any event, a miniature 3D printed robot, personalized 3D printed/laser cut items, and Team 3495 buttons.
