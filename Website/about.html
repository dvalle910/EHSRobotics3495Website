Humble Beginnings

Beginning as the humble "Robotigers" during the 2010-2011 school year, Edison High School's FIRST Robotics program was originally offered as a seventh period class, though the team now operates as a school club. Emerging from a tedious and difficult beginning, the newly-named "Mindcraft" robotics faced its second year with new intensity. Transforming from an undermanned and under-equipped team of five building out a garage into a larger, more efficient, and organized program building out of a fully-equipped dedicated classroom, the team has grown immensely. One of the early pioneers of STEM education at Edison, our program has influenced a growth in scientific interest at Edison; our successes being a foundation for the creation of our Green Academy and Bio-Medical pathways, and is directly associated with Edison's Engineering Pathway.
